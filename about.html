About

McMaster Chess Club aims to create a friendly space for players of all skill levels to explore their interests in chess. Don't be shy - most players are beginners and intermediates at the club. Everyone is welcome to come to club meetings to play games, talk, meet new people and more. There is no registration fee to join. While most meetings don't have a rigid format, we run prize tournaments every so often. Events include: chess boxing, blitz, team battles against other universities, arenas, co-hosted events with other clubs and more.

As of the Winter 2023 semester, our general meetings are hosted each Wednesday from 6:00 to 8:00 in the main Club space (MUSC room 215).

The biggest competition is when we participate in the Canadian University Chess Championships (CUCC) every year. CUCC is five rounds and sees universities across Canada compete. Our best results are tying for 4th in the championships section and winning the reserves (u-1800 section) in 2017 and 2020. We hold chess team qualifier tournaments in the first semester, and CUCC takes place in January or February. We typically send 1-2 teams (4 players each) to the tournament. In 2023, we sent 3 teams of 4 players each.
